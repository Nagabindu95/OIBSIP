Biography

Ratan Tata is a renowned industrialist and philanthropist, known for his visionary leadership and contributions to the growth of India’s economy. As the Chairman Emeritus of Tata Sons, he played a key role in transforming the Tata Group into a global business empire, with notable acquisitions like Jaguar Land Rover and Corus Steel.

Beyond business, Ratan Tata is deeply committed to social causes. His contributions to education, rural development, and healthcare have improved the lives of millions. He continues to inspire generations with his humility, compassion, and innovative spirit.
